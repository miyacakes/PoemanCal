My Poem

Roses are red,

Violets are blue,

Sugar is sweet,

And so are you.

The sun sets in the west,

The moon rises in the east,

Stars twinkle in the night sky,

A symphony of peace never ceased.

Words flow like a gentle stream,

A dance of thoughts in moonlight's gleam,

Expressing emotions, love, and dreams,

In the canvas of life, where every moment teems.
